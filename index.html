Complex System Analysis and Resilience Engineering

I am a software engineer and researcher pursuing a PhD at Lab-STICC, ENSTA, Institut Polytechnique de Paris. My research focuses on the resilience and analysis of collaborative systems, with a particular application to drone swarms. I am passionate about developing robust, intelligent, and adaptive systems that enhance coordination and reliability in complex, multi-agent environments.
